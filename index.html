Part A — Inclusion / Exclusion Criteria

COORDINATOR
Inclusion Criteria: Age 25–54 at death · Death within 14 days of presentation to MOH hospital · Diagnosed with ACS, Stroke, CKD Stage 5, or Pneumonia during terminal admission.
⚠ Exclusion Criteria — Do NOT proceed if ANY of the following apply:
  • Non-Malaysian citizen
  • Maternal mortality
  • Cases transferred from inpatient private hospitals
  • Self-inflicted injury / self-harm / violence / natural disaster
  • Active Cancer
  • Active tuberculosis on treatment (intensive / maintenance phase)
  • Patients on immunosuppressive drugs (prednisolone ≥10mg/day >28 days, or others per Lampiran A)
